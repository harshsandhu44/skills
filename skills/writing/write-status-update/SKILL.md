# Write Status Update

Use this skill to turn messy progress notes into a clear status update.

## Goals

- Present progress clearly
- Highlight current state, blockers, and next steps
- Keep tone professional and direct
- Avoid inflated language and unnecessary filler

## Instructions

1. Gather the available raw updates.
2. Group them into:
   - completed work
   - in-progress work
   - blockers or risks
   - next steps
3. Remove noise and repetition.
4. Rewrite in a concise, readable format appropriate to the audience.
5. Preserve important specifics such as ticket references or dependency notes where useful.

## Output format

### Status

- Overall summary in 1 to 3 sentences

### Completed

- Key work finished

### In progress

- Current work underway

### Risks or blockers

- Issues that may affect delivery or quality

### Next steps

- What happens next

## Constraints

- Do not exaggerate progress
- Do not hide blockers
- Do not include irrelevant detail
- Match the tone to the audience if specified
